Object detection is a computer vision technique that identifies and locates objects within images or videos. It assigns labels and bounding boxes to objects, distinguishing them from the background. This technology is crucial for applications like autonomous vehicles, surveillance, and augmented reality, where accurate object recognition and localization are essential for tasks such as obstacle detection, scene understanding, and interaction with the environment..
